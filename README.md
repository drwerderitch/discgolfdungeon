# QuestRound
disc golf adventure companion app
RULES SUMMARY

THE QUEST
You are a party of adventurers on a shared quest across 18 holes. Each hole is an encounter. The party succeeds or fails together based on average score. Par or better is a success. Over par is a failure.

TEE BOXES
Your score on the previous hole determines your tee box. Birdie or better — blue tee. Par — white tee. Bogey or worse — red tee. Everyone starts on white tee for hole one.

THE CARDS
At each tee box, before anyone throws, draw one Adventure card and read it aloud. This is your encounter for this hole.
Each birdie or better also draws a Boon card. Each bogey or worse also draws a Peril card.
Adventure cards — affect this hole. Draw at the tee, resolve before you leave. Boon cards — reward you immediately after this hole. Peril cards — condition your next hole. XP is earned immediately on draw. Tokens and items — carry until spent.

BETWEEN HOLES ENCOUNTERS
On longer walks between holes the party may optionally draw one Between Holes card. Participation is never required and skipping carries no penalty — only the potential reward is missed.

PARTY SUCCESS AND FAILURE
After everyone holes out calculate the party average score. Par or better is a success — take the success reward on the Adventure card. Over par is a failure — take the fail consequence.

GOLD
Every player earns gold individually. Gold rewards on cards pay each player that amount. Spend gold at camp after hole nine or hole eighteen to unlock new discs. A disc costs 10gp.

ADVANTAGE AND DISADVANTAGE
Advantage — throw twice, play the better lie. Disadvantage — throw twice, play the worse lie. Disadvantage cannot stack with itself. You either have it or you don't. Other conditions can stack alongside it.

ADVANTAGE TOKENS
Stored advantage tokens can be spent at any time to take advantage on any single throw or to use a triggered class passive outside of your skill tags.
Earn tokens from Boon cards, Adventure card successes, and level ups. Start with one token at character creation.
Unspent tokens at the end of a round convert to 1gp each or carry forward to the next session.

CLASSES
Every player starts at level 1 with a chosen class, one starting skill chosen from their class list, and one advantage token.
Each class has a passive ability. The passive fires for free on matching skill tagged holes. Spend a token to use a triggered passive on any other hole. Behavioral passives are always active at no cost.
Gain one skill at each level up chosen from your class list. At level 3 instead of choosing a third skill you may choose a passive from any other class or prestige class as your multiclass option. Multiclass passives have no associated skills and never fire for free. Behavioral passives are always active at no cost as a multiclass. Triggered passives always cost a token to use as a multiclass.

EXPERIENCE AND LEVELING
Drawing a Peril card earns XP immediately as printed on the card. Typically 2 XP.
Level 1 to Level 2 — 8 XP. Choose a second skill from your class list. Gain one advantage token. Level 2 to Level 3 — 9 XP. Choose a third skill or a multiclass passive. Gain one advantage token.

THE CAMP
After hole nine and after hole eighteen the party returns to camp.
Spend gold to unlock new discs at 10gp each. Spend XP to level up and choose a new skill or multiclass passive. Convert unspent advantage tokens to 1gp each or carry them forward. Buy or sell treasure items at face value if a merchant is available. Merchant class players buy at half price. Each player nominates one other player for a roleplay or character moment they enjoyed this round. That player gains 1 XP.

DISC INVENTORY
You start with a putter and one class appropriate disc. Fighters start with a putter, their chosen weapon, and one additional disc. Marksmen start with two putters and one additional disc. New discs are unlocked at camp with gold. Your physical bag is your inventory — you can only use discs you have actually unlocked. Carrying two of the same disc is a legitimate strategic choice once advantage is in play.

TREASURE ITEMS
Treasure cards are holdable magic items. Keep them until you choose to activate them. Each treasure has a printed gold value for buying and selling at merchant encounters. Reusable items recharge at camp after hole nine and after hole eighteen.

HOMEBREW CLASSES
Players are encouraged to invent their own class. Every class needs one passive ability and three skills chosen from Combat, Social, Stealth, Nature, Arcane, Physical. The group eyeballs whether it feels balanced with existing classes before approving it.

CLASSES
The passive fires for free on matching skill tagged holes. Spend a token to use a triggered passive on any other hole. Gain one skill at each level up,  chosen from your class list. Gain another skill at lvl 2
At level 3 you may choose a passive from any other class or prestige class instead of a third skill. Multiclass passives have no associated skills and never fire for free. Behavioral passives are always active at no cost. Triggered passives always cost a token to use.
SKILLS
Your class gives you skills, giving advantages on certain encounters.  If your character has a skill listed on the adventure card, you are proficient, and gain some benefit.  Benefits include greater rewards or free uses of abilities. You may choose a skill from your class list at lvl1.  Choose a second at level 2.  At level 3 you may choose a third or instead choose to multiclass or prestige class. 

FIGHTER You've thrown this disc ten thousand times. You know exactly what it does. At character creation declare your chosen weapon — one specific disc in your bag. Add it alongside your normal level one disc. You start with three discs total. Passive: Your chosen weapon cannot be taken away by disc restriction conditions. On favored holes or by spending a token, if your next throw is your chosen weapon improve your current lie up to 3 steps outside circle 2. Skills: Combat, Physical, Stealth

RANGER You know this terrain. You know which disc to reach for. Passive: Once per hole may choose any tee box regardless of earned position. Skills: Nature, Combat, Physical

DEFENDER You put yourself between the party and the danger. Passive: May declare play to mine — throws a conservative shot and the party may swap to their lie. Both the Defender and any player who swaps throw from that lie. Skills: Combat, Social, Physical

CLERIC You keep the party together when things fall apart. Passive: Grant a teammate advantage on any drive or approach without spending a token. Spending a token extends this to putts. Skills: Social, Arcane, Nature

ROGUE You wait, you watch, and you take what's there. Passive: After everyone has thrown their drive the Rogue may move to any other drive lie on the hole. Both the Rogue and the original thrower play from that spot. Skills: Stealth, Social, Combat

MERCHANT Every situation has an angle. You just have to find it. Passive: Once per hole after drawing an Adventure card may pay 1gp to redraw it. Active: Spend a token to buy any item or disc at half price at any merchant encounter or at camp. Skills: Social, Nature, Arcane

WIZARD You don't move to the disc. You move the disc to you. Passive: Once per hole, after a drive or approach lands, may move their lie up to 3 steps in any direction before throwing next. Skills: Arcane, Stealth, Social

SEER You have been here before. You will be here again. Passive: Once per hole may rethrow any drive or approach after seeing the result. Skills: Arcane, Social, Nature

MONK Still mind, still hand. You have thrown this shot before in your mind. Passive: Once per hole before your approach declare a landing zone and throw. Play that lie. Throw a second disc from the same spot — land within 10 feet of the first and gain advantage on your putt. Skills: Physical, Nature, Arcane

ASSASSIN One clean line. One clean shot. Done. Passive: If your approach lands within circle one your putt is automatic. Pick up and score. Skills: Combat, Stealth, Physical

BARD Your best moment makes everyone better. Passive: When you spend a token on yourself, declare one teammate before throwing — they also gain a stored advantage for any single throw this hole at no additional cost. Skills: Social, Nature, Arcane

BARBARIAN Glory or nothing. The long road is the only road. Passive: Before throwing you may choose to throw from blue tee regardless of your earned position. Par or better from blue tee draws a Boon. On favored holes draw two Boons instead. Skills: Combat, Physical, Social

MARKSMAN One more time. Cleaner. You start with an extra putter alongside your normal level one disc. You carry two putters from day one. Passive: When you make a putt from outside circle 2 you may attempt it a second time. If the second putt drops the party score improves by 1 for adventure success this hole. On favored holes this also applies to circle 1 putts. Skills: Combat, Physical, Stealth

DRAGOON You leap. You fall. You score. Passive: You may jump putt from anywhere outside circle 1. Finish at par or better with a jump putt and draw a Boon. On favored holes jump putts inside circle 1 are also legal. Spend a token for the same on any other hole. Skills: Combat, Physical, Nature

ARTIFICER You start with a putter and three gadget discs — your custom built toolkit. On favored holes or by spending a token you may throw any of your gadget discs freely. Otherwise limit one gadget disc per hole. Discs gained at camp are standard equipment used freely like any other class. Skills: Physical, Stealth, Arcane

ALCHEMIST Distiller of luck. Every good moment has potential. Passive: When you birdie, score par instead and bottle the extra stroke. Uncork at any time to reduce the party score by 1 for adventure success. On favored holes or by spending a token, a par earns one luck drop. Three drops fill the bottle. Skills: Nature, Arcane, Social

DRUID The forest is not your enemy. It is your home. Passive: When your disc lands in the rough, trees, or brush you may throw your scramble shot with advantage on favored holes or by spending a token. Skills: Nature, Arcane, Physical

DUELIST Steel in both hands. Never the same cut twice. Passive: You may declare a duel on any hole — alternate forehand and backhand on every throw excluding putts. Par or better on a completed duel earns 1gp. On favored holes earn 2gp instead. Skills: Combat, Physical, Stealth

PRESTIGE CLASSES Available as multiclass options at level 3 only. Disc speed restrictions apply permanently once chosen.
AGENT OF CHAOS The numbers don't lie. Only prime numbers fly. Passive: You may only throw discs whose speed rating is a prime number. On prime numbered holes gain advantage on one throw of your choice. Prestige — multiclass only.

KNIGHT OF BALANCE Order in all things. Even numbers only. Passive: You may only throw discs whose speed rating is an even number. On even numbered holes gain advantage on one throw of your choice. Prestige — multiclass only.

ADVENTURE DECK
THE TOLL KEEPER Social — Charm, Cunning
A heavyset figure blocks the trail. Arms folded. Not moving. The party has 30 seconds to agree — pay 2gp or push through. Pay: play normally. Push through: all drives with disadvantage. Success: Gain 3gp. Fail: Draw a Peril.
THE WOUNDED TRAVELER Social — Charm, Spirit
Someone on the trail, badly hurt, asking for help. Party votes before throwing: stop to help or press on. Stop: costs this hole's gp reward but draw a Boon immediately. Press on: play normally, no gp reward even on success. Success: Gain 2gp if you stopped. Nothing if you didn't. Fail: Gain 2 XP — it weighed on everyone.
THE HERMIT Social — Learning, Spirit
An old figure sits watching without expression. One player may spend 2gp to draw a Boon before throwing. Success: The hermit nods. Gain 2gp. Fail: The hermit shakes his head. Nothing.
THE WAGER Social — Cunning, Charm
A stranger offers a bet. Cocky smile. Probably a trap. Before throwing any player may wager up to 3gp. Par or better: win double the wager. Bogey or worse: lose the wager. Success: Gain 2gp. Fail: Gain 1gp — even a bad hole has lessons.
THE BANQUET Social — Charm, Learning
Word travels fast out here. Someone important is watching tonight. Before throwing any player may spend gold to make an impression — 1gp, 2gp, or 3gp each. 0-2gp total: Success earns 3gp back. 3-5gp total: Success earns 6gp back. 6gp or more: Success earns 10gp back and draw a treasure. Success: See above. Fail: Lose what you spent.
THE TRAVELING MERCHANT Social — Charm, Cunning
A trader with a full pack, heading the other direction. Before throwing any player may buy or sell treasures at face value. No wrinkle during play. Success: He throws in a bonus. Gain 2gp. Fail: He packed up quickly. No bonus.
DRAGON SIGHTED Combat — Power, Grit
A shadow passes over the canopy. Enormous. Gone in seconds. No wrinkle this hole — everyone is rattled. Success: It didn't see you. Gain 2gp. Fail: It circled back. The party loses 2gp.
THE AMBUSH Combat — Power, Cunning
They had the high ground. They planned this. The two players with the worst scores last hole throw their drives with disadvantage. Success: You fought through. Gain 3gp. Fail: You scattered. Draw a Peril. Gain 2 XP.
THE CHALLENGE Combat — Power, Grit
A warrior on the trail. He wants to test the party's best fighter. One player volunteers before throwing begins. That player must par or better this hole. Success: Gain 4gp and a stored advantage token. Fail: Gain 2 XP — they learned something the hard way.
COORDINATED ATTACK Combat — Power, Cunning
The party moves as one. No hesitation, no scouting each other's results. All throws this hole are simultaneous. Count down from three, everyone throws together. No throw may wait for another to land first. Success: Gain 3gp. Fail: Gain 1gp — you tried.
PLUNDER Combat — Power, Grit
The monster is down but the treasure is still guarded. Press your luck. After all players have holed out each may step back after each make and putt again. Miss and your run ends. Gain 1gp for each made putt. Success: Gain a bonus 2gp on top of whatever you plundered. Fail: Whatever you plundered is yours to keep regardless.
WAYSTATION Nature — Spirit, Learning
A worn campsite at the trail's edge. Someone was here recently. No wrinkle this hole. Success: The camp yields supplies. Gain 3gp. Fail: The camp is empty and cold. Gain 1gp — you needed the rest anyway.
THE GOBLIN SCOUT Combat — Cunning, Grit
Small tracks in the mud. Fresh. Heading the same direction you are. The player with the most gold must spend 1gp or throw their approach with disadvantage. Success: You caught up with it. Gain 3gp. Fail: It reported back. Draw a Peril next hole in addition to your normal draw.
OLD BONES Nature — Learning, Spirit
The remains of a campfire. Gear scattered. Someone left in a hurry. No wrinkle during play. Success: You find what they left behind. Draw a Boon. Fail: You find what came after them. Draw a Peril and gain 2 XP.
DISTANT SMOKE Nature — Learning, Spirit
A column of smoke rises from somewhere deeper in the park. Could be a camp. Could be trouble. No wrinkle this hole. Success: You press toward it boldly. Gain 3gp. Fail: You hesitate too long. Draw the next Adventure card immediately and read it aloud — that is what you find there.
STORM THREATENING Nature — Grit, Power
The sky has gone the wrong color. The wind is picking up. All putts this hole must be made twice consecutively to count. Miss the second and add a stroke. Success: The storm passes. Gain 2gp. Fail: The storm arrives. All putts next hole must also be made twice to count.
THE LONG VIEW Nature — Learning, Cunning
From the high ground you can see the whole valley. The party may look at the top two Adventure cards and choose which comes next. Success: The view steadies you. Gain 2gp. Fail: What you see ahead looks hard. The unchosen card goes on top instead.
THE RIVER CROSSING Physical — Power, Grit
The trail ends at the water. No bridge. No easy way around. Before throwing the party chooses: wade through or find another way. Wade: everyone throws from one tee box lower than earned this hole. Find another way: everyone throws from one tee box higher than earned. Success: Gain 3gp. Fail: Gain 1gp — you made it across but it cost you.
THE OFFERING STONE Arcane — Spirit, Charm
A flat rock at the trailside. Coins and small tokens left by travelers before you. Any player may leave 1gp as an offering before throwing. If at least two players offer: the party gains two stored advantage tokens regardless of score. Success: The offering was worthy. Gain those tokens plus 1gp back each. Fail: The tokens remain. Something noted your generosity.
THE STANDING STONES Arcane — Spirit, Learning
Ancient markers arranged in a pattern nobody remembers. Before throwing the party may spend 2gp to gain two stored advantage tokens — the stones respond to offering. No wrinkle during play. Success: The stones pulse faintly. Gain 2gp. Fail: The stones are cold and silent. Nothing.
THE WRONG PATH Arcane — Learning, Cunning
Something redirected you here. You didn't choose this route. One player chosen by the group draws a Peril card and reads it aloud but does not apply it yet — it activates only if the party fails this hole. Success: The path corrects itself. Discard the Peril unresolved. Fail: It was leading you here all along. Apply the Peril now.
THE FAIRY RING Arcane — Spirit, Cunning
Something small and bright darts between the trees. It stops. It noticed something. If any player is carrying a treasure item: that player throws their drive with disadvantage — the fairy is drawn to the magic and gives away your position. If nobody is carrying a treasure item: the fairy leads you somewhere lucky. Draw a Boon. Success: Gain 2gp. Fail: Gain 1gp.
THE RUINS Arcane — Learning, Cunning
Stone walls half standing, something still inside worth having. No wrinkle during play. Success: You search carefully and find something useful. Draw a treasure card. Fail: Whatever is inside heard you coming. Draw a Peril.
THE CURSED LIE Arcane — Grit, Spirit
Something about this spot feels wrong. The ground, the air, the light. One player volunteers before throwing begins. That player throws their approach twice and plays the worse lie. Success: They make par anyway. Gain a stored advantage token and 2 XP. Fail: They bogey. Disadvantage on their drive next hole.
THE WATCHERS Stealth — Cunning, Grit
Eyes in the treeline. Not animals. Not friendly. To avoid detection each player must choose one disc before the hole begins and use it for every throw including putts. Success: They did not move. Gain 2gp. Fail: They moved. The party loses 2gp.
THE DECOY Stealth — Cunning, Power
One player draws attention so the others can slip through. Before throwing one player volunteers as the decoy. They must throw their fastest disc. All other players must throw their slowest. Success: The distraction worked. Gain 3gp. Fail: It drew the wrong kind of attention. The decoy player draws a personal Peril.
THROUGH THE SHADOWS Stealth — Cunning, Grit
The only way through is clean. No crashing through brush, no rattling the trees. Any player whose throw hits an obstacle — tree, bush, anything audible — draws a personal Peril immediately. Any player who lands clean draws a personal Boon at the end of the hole. Success: Gain 3gp. Fail: Gain 1gp.
CLEAR SKIES (x2) Nature — Spirit, Grit
Good conditions. Good ground. A clean hole. No wrinkle this hole. Success: Gain 2gp. Fail: Gain 1gp — even a bad hole on a good day has something in it.

BETWEEN HOLES DECK
On holes with enough distance between them, the party may draw from this deck. 
PROVISIONS (x2) The party needs to eat tonight. Everyone contributes what they can. Each player chooses one task before moving on: Find something edible or useful — a berry, a mushroom, an herb, anything the woods might offer. Call a shot — name a target and hit it with your putter. The quarry is yours. Identify something — name a tree, a bird, a plant you can see. Make fire — find tinder, dry leaves, anything that would burn. Collect it. Each completed task earns the whole party 1gp and 1 XP. Tasks judged by group consensus.

SPOUT LORE (x2) The trail levels out. There's a moment to breathe. Weave a connection between the adventures you've had. Each player who contributes to the story gains 1 XP.

THE STUCK WAGON The cart is bogged down. Keep it moving. Choose one disc as the wagon. The party rolls it together from this tee to the next — pass it between players, redirect it around obstacles, keep it rolling. If it reaches the next tee without stopping everyone gains 1gp.

SAFE PASSAGE Don't drop it. The party tosses one disc between players ultimate style while walking to the next tee. If everyone touches it at least once and nobody drops it everyone gains 1 XP.

CALLED SHOT — BACKHAND Name your target. Backhand only. Each player names a specific target and throws backhand. Every player who hits their called target gains 1gp.

CALLED SHOT — FOREHAND Name your target. Forehand only. Each player names a specific target and throws forehand. Every player who hits their called target gains 1gp.

CLOSEST TO THE PIN — BACKHAND Find a natural target and mark a throwing line. Backhand only. Everyone throws backhand. Closest gains 1gp.

CLOSEST TO THE PIN — FOREHAND Find a natural target and mark a throwing line. Forehand only. Everyone throws forehand. Closest gains 1gp.

SWOOPING ATTACK Something large descends from above. Drive it back. Take turns — one player launches a disc straight up while one other player tries to hit it before it lands. Rotate until everyone has had a turn throwing. Each successful hit earns that player 1gp.

PERILOUS TERRAIN The path ahead is treacherous. Keep your gear steady. Each player balances a disc on one fingertip for the walk to the next tee. Drop it and lose 1gp. Make it and your gear arrives unscathed.

PERIL DECK (20 cards)
CORNERED No cover. No good options. Just you and the consequences. The player who triggered this Peril throws their next drive from blue tee regardless of earned position. Redemption: make par anyway. Gain 2 XP. Tags: Combat — Power, Grit

THE DEBT COLLECTOR Someone remembers what you took. They want it back now. The player who triggered this Peril must spend 2gp immediately or throw this hole's drives and approaches with disadvantage. Redemption: make par anyway. Gain 2 XP. Tags: Social — Charm, Cunning

CURSED HANDS The disc feels wrong. Every disc feels wrong. The player who triggered this Peril must use only their putter for the entire hole. Redemption: make par anyway. Gain 2 XP. Tags: Arcane — Spirit, Grit

SPOOKED A sound in the trees. Close. Wrong. The player who triggered this Peril throws their next approach with disadvantage. Redemption: make par anyway. Gain 2 XP. Tags: Stealth — Cunning, Grit

LOST ON THE TRAIL Wrong turn. Wrong hill. Nothing looks familiar. The whole party throws drives with disadvantage this hole. Redemption: party average par or better anyway. Gain 2 XP each. Tags: Nature — Grit, Learning

THE WEIGHT OF IT Bad decisions compound. The trail gets harder. The whole party throws their approaches with disadvantage this hole. Redemption: party average par or better anyway. Gain 2 XP each. Tags: Nature — Grit, Power

HEXED Something followed you from the last hole. It found a home in your bag. The player who triggered this Peril must discard one treasure item if they have one, or spend 3gp if they don't. Redemption: none. The hex takes what it wants. Tags: Arcane — Spirit, Learning

EYES CLOSED The path ahead is unclear. You have to feel your way through. The player who triggered this Peril throws their drive with their eyes closed. Redemption: land it in the fairway anyway. Gain 3 XP. Tags: Physical — Grit, Power

DEADWEIGHT Something is slowing you down. You can feel it. The player who triggered this Peril may only use their two slowest discs this hole. Redemption: make par anyway. Gain 2 XP. Tags: Physical — Grit, Cunning

THE UNRAVELING Nothing is working. Everything feels off. The player who triggered this Peril must make one throw of their choice this hole with their non-dominant hand. They choose which throw. Redemption: make par anyway. Gain 3 XP. Tags: Physical — Grit, Power

UNFINISHED BUSINESS It doesn't count until it's certain. Do it again. The player who triggered this Peril must sink two consecutive putts for their score to count. Miss either one and add a stroke. Redemption: sink both cleanly. Gain 2 XP. Tags: Combat — Grit, Power

WORST DRIVE (x3) The party plays from the worst drive this hole instead of their own lie. Tags: Party

RATTLED (x3) The player who triggered this Peril throws one shot of their choice this hole with disadvantage. Tags: Personal

SETBACK (x3) The party loses 2gp. Tags: Party
